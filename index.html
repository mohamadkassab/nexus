Nexus Kitchen Group connects manufacturers, people, and ideas.

Under one roof, Nexus Kitchen Group connects manufacturers, people, and ideas. Under one roof, complementary brands share experience, technology, and opportunity, so each can grow faster and stronger while staying true to its craft. Our model is simple: we connect factories to empower growth. We combine product ranges and know-how, apply economies of scale, and keep quality at the center. We believe in being close to the markets we serve, investing locally, training local teams, and building foundations that lasts. This is how we create professional kitchen solutions that are reliable, beautiful, and efficient, and how we turn collaboration into real progress.

Collaboration

We connect complementary strengths, align roadmaps, and share what works. Openness accelerates progress, reduces duplication, and lets each brand focus on what it does best while the whole system moves forward together.

Sustainability

We aim to reduce waste and miles, prioritize durable engineering, and build supply closer to demand. Local production, efficient logistics, and long-life products are the foundation for responsible growth.

Shared Knowledge

Expertise grows when it is transferred. We document, teach, and mentor across teams and countries, so skills, standards, and improvements are replicated consistently and raise the bar for everyone.

Craftsmanship

Quality is non-negotiable. From materials to assembly, from testing to finish, we treat every product as a promise, built to perform and designed to serve chefs and operators day after day.

Future Factories Vision

We plan and invest for tomorrow, modular lines, digital solutions, smarter energy use, and teams trained to evolve with technology The goal is infrastructure that is local, adaptive, and ready to scale.

Expertise

Our leadership team brings together decades of experience in professional kitchen manufacturing, project execution, and operational excellence , united by a shared purpose: to engineer growth through collaboration and build a global manufacturing alliance.

Our Team

Behind every brand is a team of specialists, engineers, and leaders who focus on their own expertise while working side by side on shared projects. heir combined knowledge and dedication turn every project into a reflection of the group’s collective excellence.

NORTH

Refrigeration

With over 17 years of combined industry experience and close ties to European suppliers, North has grown from a humble warehouse into one of the region’s largest foaming and jig facilities, manufacturing its entire refrigeration line in-house. Its products are engineered for performance, durability, and simplicity, offering premium Italian components assembled in the UAE.

KOPA

Grilling Solutions

Founded in 2014 in Slovenia, with advanced production facilities spanning over 6,000 square meters, equipped with TRUMPF laser cutters, robotic welding, and CNC machining centers, Kopa combines traditional grilling techniques with cutting-edge engineering, ensuring that every grill is tailored to the chef’s unique vision, delivering efficient, reliable, and visually striking cooking solutions.

AVANTE

Ventilation Systems

With over 50 years of collective engineering experience across the UK, the Gulf, and India, Avante delivers innovative ventilation, filtration, and fire protection solutions. Based in Dubai Investment Park 2 in the UAE, Avante’s 20,000 sq. ft. facility ensures full in-house production, stringent quality standards, and custom-engineered systems in kitchen extract ventilation, HVAC, demand-based ventilation, and fire suppression.

DARC

Cold Creations

DARC produces state-of-the-art refrigerated display units that preserve food to perfection while forming an integral part of a venue’s interior design. With 17 years of refrigeration expertise, DARC is dedicated to innovation in design, craftsmanship, and customization. The company offers high-end showcases that combine performance and outstanding aesthetics.

KOOK

Cooking Suites

Kook is a Slovenian manufacturer specializing in bespoke cooking suites and stainless steel fabrication that harmonize with the Front of House space. Kook combines 20 years of expertise with each suite designed to merge innovation, design precision, and performance, delivering professional kitchen blocks that unite all cooking technologies into a seamless monoblock system for the ultimate culinary experience.

KARV

Outdoor Living

Karv focuses on outdoor living solutions that combine craftsmanship, design, and functionality. Born in Slovenia and shaped by years of expertise in professional grilling solutions, Karv has evolved into a symbol of refined outdoor living. Karv unites European precision with Middle Eastern vision, redefining the outdoors as a place for connection, celebration, and timeless elegance.
