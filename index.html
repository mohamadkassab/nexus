Nexus Kitchen Group connects manufacturers, people, and ideas.

Under one roof, Nexus Kitchen Group connects manufacturers, people, and ideas. Under one roof, complementary brands share experience, technology, and opportunity, so each can grow faster and stronger while staying true to its craft. Our model is simple: we connect factories to empower growth. We combine product ranges and know-how, apply economies of scale, and keep quality at the center. We believe in being close to the markets we serve, investing locally, training local teams, and building foundations that lasts. This is how we create professional kitchen solutions that are reliable, beautiful, and efficient, and how we turn collaboration into real progress.

Collaboration

We connect complementary strengths, align roadmaps, and share what works.
Openness accelerates progress, reduces duplication,
and lets each brand focus on what it does best while the whole system moves forward together.

Sustainability

We aim to reduce waste and miles, prioritize durable engineering, and build supply closer to demand.
Local production, efficient logistics,
and long-life products are the foundation for responsible growth.

Shared Knowledge

Expertise grows when it is transferred. We document, teach,
and mentor across teams and countries, so skills, standards, and improvements
are replicated consistently and raise the bar for everyone.

Craftsmanship

Quality is non-negotiable. From materials to assembly,
from testing to finish, we treat every product as a promise,
built to perform and designed to serve chefs and operators day after day.

Future Factories Vision

We plan and invest for tomorrow, modular lines, digital solutions,
smarter energy use, and teams trained to evolve with technology
The goal is infrastructure that is local, adaptive, and ready to scale.

Expertise

Our leadership team brings together decades of experience in professional kitchen manufacturing, project execution, and operational excellence , united by a shared purpose: to engineer growth through collaboration and build a global manufacturing alliance.

OUR TEAM

Behind every brand is a team of specialists, engineers, and leaders who focus on their own expertise while working side by side on shared projects. Their combined knowledge and dedication turn every project into a reflection of the group’s collective excellence.

The versatile and
unlimited refrigeration
